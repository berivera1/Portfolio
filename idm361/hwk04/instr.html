This is a local multiplayer game meant for 2 players.

Player 1: ○ Player 2: ●

On your turn, you can move one piece one spot in any cardinal direction


OR you can attack one piece one spot in any cardinal or diagonal direction

When moving a piece, the spot they moved from caves in.

If an area is surrounded by caved-in spots and only contains pieces from one team, it all caves in.

Conquer all of your opponent's pieces to win!



Turn your device to PORTRAIT to play this game!
